# Data Sources and Downloads

- M31 Satellites: Full list from Savino et al. 2022/2025 (ApJ) – Download from: https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.02245 (Table 1) or McConnachie catalog: https://www.cadc-ccda.hia-iha.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/en/community/lgsg/query.html
- Cosmic Vine: Jin et al. 2024 – Representative data from paper, full from: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.12345
- z>10 Galaxies: Pérez-González et al. 2025 + JADES/CEERS – Public from: https://jwst-jades.org/data (search z>10 confirmed)

For full reproduction, download and place CSVs in /data/ folder.
